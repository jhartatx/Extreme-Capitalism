EXTREME CAPITALISM:

Extreme Capitalism is a game played by four players. Like many games, each person has his own game token that he moves on the board. The goal of this game is to be the last person with most money at the end of the game.


Rules of The Game:

  1. Whoever logs onto the game first to play will be the player to start. It will then go in order of the players to log on second, third, and fourth that will determine their order in the game. -- You will notice on the left side of the page there is a navigation bar the players in descending order.

  2. Player 1 will click the “Roll The Dice” button on the game board to begin the game. His/her first roll will begin the game and move his/her according piece on the game board.

  3. At the end of your turn. Please click the “End Your Turn” button on the game board to allow the next player to begin his/her turn.

  4. At the end of of the first player's turn, player two may take their turn and so on and so forth till player four completes his turn by clicking the “End Your Turn” button on the game board, at which it will be the first player's turn once again.

  5. Whenever you land on a property that no one owns, you can buy it from the bank. To buy the property, click the “Purchase This Property” button on the game board. -- If you do not wish to buy the property you have landed on, it will go up for auction in the “Auction House” button. This button is located on the menu bar on the top right hand side of the page and is marked by this icon

  6. Auction House -- People may place their bids for the property. You may bid on property by first clicking the “Post” button, then inputting a number value in the text field and clicking the “Bid” button. Once you click “Bid” button you have 30 seconds to be the highest bidder for the property, If someone else bids a higher number it will appear in the text input field, at which time you may bid higher or let the time run out for which the other player with the highest bid will receive the property. If you are in the last 10 seconds of the 30 second timer and you place a higher bid, the timer will restart the last 10 seconds so other players have the opportunity to bid if they so wish. You will be notified when you have received the property.

  7. All of the given prices for the properties are on the game board. Once you own the land, players must pay a rent fee if they land on your property. If you land on YOUR property, you will NOT be charged that fee.

  8. If you land on a Chance or a Community Chest card, you must do what it says. For example if it says “Go To Jail” your piece will automatically move to the “Jail” tile to fulfill the corresponding card action.

  9. If you roll doubles (the same number on both dice) you game piece will take its first turn and then you will get to roll the dice again. However, if you roll doubles three times in a row you must go to jail.

  10. When you pass go, you will automatically collect $200 from the bank.

  11. If you land on “Free Parking” you will automatically collect $500 from the bank.

  12. Jail- There are three ways to get into jail 1) you land on the space labeled “Go to Jail” 2). You pick a Chance or Community Chest card that says “Go to Jail” or 3) you roll doubles three times. And there are also three ways to get out of jail: 1) you get three turns to roll a double, if you do not roll a double in the three turns you must pay the fine 2) using a “Get out of Jail Free” card (can be found in Chance or Community Chest), 3) pay a fine of £50.

  13. Once you own all of one color properties, you can choose to upgrade your property value by paying a fee. By upgrading your property, the next time another player lands on it, you will receive a larger set sum of money then if it were to be a regular property.

  14. Bankruptcy. If you are bankrupt, you cannot pay someone rent or cannot pay a tax. If you declare bankruptcy you have officially lost the game.

User Instructions:


There are four main components of the board.

  1. The Left Hand User Panel
  2. The Game Board
  3. The Top Right Navigation Menu Bar
  4. The Chat Box

The Left Hand User Panel

The left hand user panel consists of all the player in the current game being held. The order of the players in the game (player 1, player 2, player 3, player 4) is congruent will the descending order of the players listed out in this left hand panel. To view player information including what game piece they are playing with, how much money they currently have in the bank, and what properties they own, you can click on the players tab and a drop down menu will display the information. To hide the drop down menu, click the same players panel to hide. You may view any players statistics at any time.


The Game Board

The game board is located in the center of the screen. To view the game rules please click the “Game Rules” button in the navigation bar in the top right navigation bar marked with this icon


The Top Right Navigation Menu Bar

The top right navigation bar consists of the following options

  1. = GAME RULES: Game Rules consists of all of the player game rules for the digital board game Extreme Capitalism
  2. = USER INFORMATION: The current information you are viewing consisting of basic game functions and user information.
  3. = AUCTION HOUSE: The auction house button allows you to place bids for properties and compete against your fellow players to be the highest bidder to win the property.
  4. = END GAME: This button is specifically designed to end the game all together. This will not only take you out of the game but also all your fellow players as well.

The Comment Box

The Chat Box is located on the bottom right hand side of the page. Not only will you be able to communicate back and forth with your fellow players, but Extreme Capitalism Man will also keep you updated with each individual's players moves as well. To minimize the chat box, simply click on the top dark gray bar. To enlarge it, click on the same top dark gray bar once more.
